About Trinetra

Trinetra Digital Labs is an independent technology initiative led by Ravindra Kumar Shori, an individual proprietor based in Mumbai. Inspired by the concept of the Trinetra — the Third Eye representing vision, intelligence and awareness, the initiative focuses on developing modern digital systems powered by artificial intelligence and intelligent automation.
